When writing job applications I am always keeping these business mindset:

1. What is the company's mission?
2. What is the product?
3. Who are the competitors?
4. Who are the customers?

When possible I always try to implement this CPCC framework when writing cover letters. This is in addition to always thinking how I can bring values to them. I think this can boost success rate. Testing it out..
