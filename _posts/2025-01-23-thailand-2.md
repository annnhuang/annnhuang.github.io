## Lunch at Sri Trat Restaurant and Bar

This resturant serves the best Thai cuisine!! We had a great time there. The restaurant is also [Michelin-starred](https://guide.michelin.com/th/en/article/dining-out/8-michelin-listed-bangkok-restaurants-with-impressive-bars)

📍[90 Sukhumvit 33 Alley, Khlong Tan Nuea, Watthana, Bangkok 10110, Thailand](https://maps.app.goo.gl/YtFfhkuJUAAmjC5G7)
